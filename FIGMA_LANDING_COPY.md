# Sigil Landing Page Copy Handoff

## Project Context
- **Brand:** Sigil
- **Page Type:** Marketing landing page
- **Status Positioning:** Live now
- **Primary Goal:** Convert two audiences on one page:
  - Supporters who want to fund strong builders
  - Developers who want to get funded without becoming full-time community managers

## Voice & Tone
- Clear, practical, and confident
- Builder-first language
- Avoid hype, vague claims, and buzzwords
- Keep trust high: simple, direct, verifiable language

## Writing Principles for Design
- Headlines should be short and legible in hero layouts
- Body copy should read clearly in 2-4 line blocks
- Use explicit action verbs in CTAs
- Keep supporter and developer pathways equally visible
